Frequently Asked Questions

How does the referral program work?

When you download the app for each friend you refer,you get one entry into the quiz. Once you reach 3 lifetime referrals, the quiz entry is free for you forever.Each subsequent referral will get you a life that can be used to revive yourself during a quiz, in case you answer incorrectly.

I have won a quiz when can i expect my money?

All prize money is delivered into your account in 2-3 working days.

There are many investing and educational apps, why should i choose TradeRoyale?

Learning is awesome and fun, but definitely nothing beats earning while you learn. Our various features such as quiz and contests encourage individuals to test and hone their skills before entering the ruthless capital markets

What is TradeRoyale?

Simple! To increase the financial literacy and bring in more people into the world of investing. We believe this can be achieved only by incentivizing the end user.
